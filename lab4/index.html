What is SiIvaGunner?

SiIvaGunner is a music parody group on YouTube that uploads videos posing as video game soundtracks, when in reality they're edited in different ways to sound different from the original music. This could include changing the melody of the original song to another song, or taking the existing music and mashing it up with a pop song, or other media.

Overreach

The SiIvaGunner YouTube channel currently has music edits created by hundreds of anonymous contributors posing as a single person. The channel currently has over 400K subscribers, but possibly more viewers due to the channels consistent and extremely often upload schedule. The channel has created many tributes to various video games, composers, bands, and other popular media.
