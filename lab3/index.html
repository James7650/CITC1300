My Favorite Video Games

Rhythm Heaven

Rhythm Heaven is definitely one of my most favorite video games of all time, it's really what got myself interested in music in the first place, and as a series every game is very close to me.

Super Mario Bros. 3

As a big fan of Super Mario games, if I had to choose a favorite, it would most likely be Super Mario Bros. 3. It's such a fun game to replay over and over, definitely a defining game for many different platformers.

Pikmin 2

The Pikmin series is all about strategy and growth, and I feel the second game, Pikmin 2, was the peak of the series. The game focuses more on an overarching adventure rather than the emphasis on replayability the other games have, and this works the most in its favor.
